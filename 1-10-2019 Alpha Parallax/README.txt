Controls

On Keyboard
'A' 'D' - move left and right respectively
'Space' - jump/ Hold to jump higher
'F' - bring up elemental ability. Press again to place it
'Up' 'Down' 'Left' 'Right' (Arrow Keys) - move elemental ability

On XBox 360 Controller
Left joystick - move left and right
'A' or '3' - jump/ Hold to jump higher
'B' or '2' - bring up elemental ability. Press again to place it
Right joystick - move elemental ability when created
'Select' or 'Reset' - restart the level
