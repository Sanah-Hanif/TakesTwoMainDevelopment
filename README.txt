Controls

On XBox 360 Controller
Left joystick - move left and right
'A' or '3' - jump/ Hold to jump higher
'B' or '2' - bring up elemental ability. Press again to place it
'Y' or '1' - cancel an elemental ability before it is placed
Right joystick - move elemental ability when created
'Select' or 'Reset' - pause the game

Known Glitches
- parallax background sometimes appears in front of the exit gateways and the nodules on the door, switches and pressure pads
